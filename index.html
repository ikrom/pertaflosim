We Have Many Features

PVT Analysis

PVT Analysis is a module to analyze fluid properties. The fluid types are dry gas, black oil, water, and compositional fluid.

Surface Model

Gas Transmission.
Gas Transmission is a module to simulate the pressure distribution and determine flow direction on the gas transmission pipeline network.

Multiphase.
Multiphase is a module to simulate the pressure and temperature distribution of mixed oil, water, and gas flow through network pipeline within the non-isothermal and steady-state condition. This module has 2 sub-modules (Multiphase Black Oil and Multiphase Compositional).

Distribution Network.
Distribution Network is a module to simulate the pressure distribution and determine flow direction on isothermal gas distribution linear or looping pipeline network. This module has 2 sub-modules (Dry Gas Distribution Network and Compositional Distribution Network).

Subsurface Model

IPR & Well Modelling.
IPR & Well Modelling is a module to simulate two-phase fluid flow from the reservoir, through the formation, and into the vertical well. They are expressed in IPR curve as the BHP and flow rate.
