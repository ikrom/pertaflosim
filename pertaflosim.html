We Have Many Features

PVT Analysis

PVT Analysis is used to analyze fluid properties. The fluid types are dry gas, black oil, water, and compositional fluid.

Surface Model

Gas Transmission. Used to simulate the pressure distribution and determine flow direction on gas transmission pipeline network

Multiphase.Tool to simulate the pressure and temperature distribution of mixed oil, water, and gas flow through network pipeline within non-isothermal and steady state condition

Distribution Network.Modul to simulate the pressure distribution and determine flow direction on isothermal gas distribution linear or looping pipeline network

Subsurface Model

IPR & Well Modelling.Simulate two phase fluid flow from the reservoir, through the formation, and into the vertical well. They are expressed in IPR curve as the BHP and flow rate
